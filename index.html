Selected Works

What this looks like in practice.

+

Turning fragmented data into a $700k capital recovery.

TCCI Manufacturing • Finance Operations

The Problem

Finance teams had years of import/export data scattered across folders. $700K in eligible duty drawback refunds sat unclaimed because the data wasn't organized.

What I Did

Built a 4-stage data pipeline: OCR extraction, standardized formatting, Python-based cleaning, and automated government submission. Understood the real bottleneck wasn't filing—it was data structure.

Impact

Recovered $700K in the first claim ever filed. Created a repeatable system that scales across countries and time periods.

+

Ending the era of paper-based operations.

TCCI Manufacturing • Operations Systems

The Problem

Operations across production, quality, and warehouse were paper-based. Data was fragmented, reporting was delayed, and management had no visibility into what was happening.

What I Did

Interviewed stakeholders across all teams. Designed standardized data capture. Built multiple Power Apps with SharePoint as data layer and Power Automate for workflow automation.

Impact

Digitized 80%+ of operational processes. Reduced manual effort. Enabled real-time visibility without relying on manual status updates.

+

Turning low ratings into product improvements.

Amazon • Fire TV Cube • Post-launch quality

The Problem

After Fire TV Cube launched, customer ratings dropped. Feedback was scattered across thousands of reviews with no clear signal on what was driving the decline.

What I Did

Analyzed large volumes of customer reviews to identify patterns and separate recurring issues from one-off complaints. Reproduced reported issues to validate root causes and ensure findings reflected real user problems. Identified a firmware mismatch issue — remotes with older firmware were failing to pair with devices running the latest OS — creating high-friction setup. Mapped the issue to the onboarding journey, identifying it as a critical failure point in first-time user experience.

Impact

Enabled teams to prioritize fixes including firmware updates and replacement remotes for affected users. Helped resolve a key source of customer frustration and contributed to improving product ratings from 3.0 to 4.0+.

+

Catching what matters before users do.

Amazon • Fire TV • Release quality

The Problem

Product releases often carried issues that only surfaced after launch, impacting customer experience and increasing post-release fixes.

What I Did

Led cross-team bug bash sessions and coordinated beta testing to identify high-impact issues before release. Defined focus areas for testing based on product changes and user impact, ensuring teams prioritized what mattered most. Worked with engineering teams to translate findings into actionable fixes before launch.

Impact

Improved release readiness by surfacing critical issues earlier in the development cycle. Reduced post-release defects and enabled more informed release decisions.

+

Making approvals move at the speed of work.

TCCI Manufacturing • Process Design

The Problem

Work requests lived in Microsoft Planner with no standardized format. Approvals took 3-5 days. Management had zero visibility. Teams were frustrated.

What I Did

Designed a workflow-driven system with structured intake, approval gates, and real-time visibility. Used Power Apps, SharePoint, and Power Automate to enforce process discipline.

Impact

Approval time dropped 60% (3-5 days → 1-2 days). Manual follow-ups reduced 80%. Management now sees real-time status without asking.

Working Together

How I usually plug in.

1. Listen & Diagnose
