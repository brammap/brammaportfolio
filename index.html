Duty Drawback Claims Automation

TCCI Manufacturing $700K Impact

The Problem

Finance team had years of import/export data scattered across folders with no centralized system. They wanted to "automate duty drawback claims," but the real bottleneck wasn't filing—it was that nobody had ever organized the underlying data. $700K in eligible refunds were sitting there, unclaimed.

What I Did

I designed a 4-stage data pipeline: OCR extraction of documents, standardized data formatting, Python-based cleaning and validation, and automated government submission workflows. By understanding the real problem first, I built a solution that unlocked $700K in recovered value.

Impact

Finance team recovered the first-ever duty drawback claims. Process is now scalable across multiple countries, time periods, and shipment volumes. This isn't just a one-time win—it's a repeatable system.

Work Order Management System

TCCI Manufacturing 60% Faster Approvals

The Problem

Work requests were managed through manual entries in Microsoft Planner with no standardized format. Management had zero visibility, approvals were informal, and approval-to-execution took 3-5 days. Teams were frustrated. Leadership was blind.

What I Did

I listened to operations, lab, quality, and management teams. The issue wasn't Planner—it was the lack of a control layer governing intake, approvals, and scheduling. I designed a workflow-driven system using Power Apps, SharePoint, and Power Automate that created structured intake, approval gates, and real-time management visibility.

Impact

Approval-to-scheduling time reduced from 3-5 days to 1-2 days (60% faster). Manual status updates reduced by 80%. Real-time management visibility without relying on manual follow-ups.

Operations Digitization Platform

TCCI Manufacturing 80% Process Digitization

The Problem

Operational workflows across production, quality, and warehouse teams were manual and paper-based. Teams relied on check-sheets, Excel files, and ad-hoc tracking, leading to fragmented data, delayed reporting, and heavy dependency on manual follow-ups. Management lacked real-time visibility into operations.

What I Did

Worked with stakeholders across production, quality, and warehouse teams to understand how workflows operated and identify inefficiencies. Designed a structured approach to standardize data capture and workflow management. Built multiple applications using Power Apps with SharePoint as the data layer and Power Automate for workflow automation.

Impact

Digitized over 80% of operational processes across multiple teams. Reduced manual effort and improved data consistency. Enabled real-time visibility into operations without relying on manual updates.
