Capabilities

Product Strategy & Delivery

I specialize in cleaning up unstructured data environments and building the logic required to ensure that leadership makes decisions based on reality rather than assumptions.

Selected Works

What this looks like in practice.

+

Turning fragmented data into a $700k capital recovery.

TCCI Manufacturing • Finance Operations

The Problem

Finance teams had years of import/export data scattered across folders. $700K in eligible duty drawback refunds sat unclaimed because the data wasn't organized.

What I Did

Built a 4-stage data pipeline: OCR extraction, standardized formatting, Python-based cleaning, and automated government submission. Understood the real bottleneck wasn't filing—it was data structure.

Impact

Recovered $700K in the first claim ever filed. Created a repeatable system that scales across countries and time periods.

+

Making approvals move at the speed of work.

TCCI Manufacturing • Process Design

The Problem

Work requests lived in Microsoft Planner with no standardized format. Approvals took 3-5 days. Management had zero visibility. Teams were frustrated.

What I Did

Designed a workflow-driven system with structured intake, approval gates, and real-time visibility. Used Power Apps, SharePoint, and Power Automate to enforce process discipline.

Impact

Approval time dropped 60% (3-5 days → 1-2 days). Manual follow-ups reduced 80%. Management now sees real-time status without asking.

+

Making mentorship less dependent on luck.

Women in Product Hackathon 2026 • 🏆 Winner

The Problem

Existing mentorship platforms match on surface criteria (company, industry) while ignoring what actually determines great mentorship: aligned goals and compatible experience levels.

What I Did

Conducted user research with 10+ mentors and mentees. Designed a matching framework that asks the right questions. Built a functional prototype in one week.

Impact

Won the hackathon. Selected for community implementation. Proved that thoughtful product thinking beats feature shipping.

+

Ending the era of paper-based operations.

TCCI Manufacturing • Operations Systems

The Problem

Operations across production, quality, and warehouse were paper-based. Data was fragmented, reporting was delayed, and management had no visibility into what was happening.

What I Did

Interviewed stakeholders across all teams. Designed standardized data capture. Built multiple Power Apps with SharePoint as data layer and Power Automate for workflow automation.

Impact

Digitized 80%+ of operational processes. Reduced manual effort. Enabled real-time visibility without relying on manual status updates.

Working Together

How I usually plug in.

1. Listen & Diagnose

It starts with what hurts. You walk me through the workflow, product, or system, and I talk with the people closest to the work. Together we map what's actually broken and what we're really solving.

2. Design & Prototype

I turn that understanding into clear structures, flows, and designs. Prototypes, system diagrams, or Figma—at the right fidelity so we can react to something concrete and decide together.

3. Build & Refine

Once we like a direction, I stay involved through the first versions. We get it built, add measurement so we learn from it, and refine based on how real people actually use it.

FAQs

Questions people usually ask.

+ When does working together make sense?

When you have complex workflows that need fixing, you're not sure where the real bottleneck is, or you need someone who can go from problem diagnosis through shipped solution. I work best with teams that care about actually solving the problem vs just shipping features.

+ What industries have you worked with?

Most of my work has been in manufacturing operations, fintech, and product platforms. I've built internal tools, automated workflows, and platform features. The industry matters less than the problem—if it's about taking friction out of complex work, I can help.

+ How do you usually work with teams?

Depends on the stage. Early on, I'm asking questions, interviewing users, mapping flows, and prototyping. Once we like a direction, I stay hands-on through build and launch, working closely with your engineers and stakeholders to make sure we ship something real.

+ Can you go into more detail about your work?

Sure. I've recovered $700K in hidden value by structuring unorganized trade data. I've reduced approval cycles from 3-5 days to 1-2 days by designing standardized workflows. And I won a hackathon by building a mentorship matching system under time pressure. Each shows a different aspect of how I approach problems.

+ What about emerging tech and AI?

I'm interested in how AI actually helps with real workflows—not hype. OCR automation for data processing, ML for pattern detection, LLMs for content generation. I focus on whether it solves a real problem vs creates new ones.
